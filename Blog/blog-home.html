Welcome to my Blog!

My name is Ivan Raffucci, and again welcome! I'm just a guy who loves coding and web devleopment and felt compelled to start writing about it online. Here, I talk about my experiences learning web development with no background and entirely free! (okay I bought one udemy course, but come on, who can say no to a $10 course??) Either way, I am here to learn to be a better programmer and to log my experiences as I do so. It has been an amazing time so far learning to code. There have been a lot of challenges and plenty of time spent staring in confusion at my computer screen, but it is overcoming those moments that drives me to keep going and in writing these posts, I hope to log each of those roadblocks and eventual successes and maybe teach others along the way!
